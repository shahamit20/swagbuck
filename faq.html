Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions.

How do I earn SB Coins?

+

You can earn SE Coins by smiling for detection, completing surveys, and referring friends.

How do I redeem my rewards?

+

Go to the redeem page, select your preferred reward (Amazon, Flipkart, or PayPal), and confirm your redemption.

Can I use Swagbucks on my mobile device?

+

Yes, Swagbucks is mobile-friendly and can be accessed through a web browser on your smartphone or tablet.

How does smile recognition work on Swagbucks?

+

Swagbucks uses AI-powered facial recognition to detect smiles and reward users with SE Coins when they smile.

Is my data safe with Swagbucks?

+

Yes, we prioritize user privacy and security. We do not store facial recognition data or share personal details without consent.

What happens if my smile is not detected?

+

If your smile is not detected, ensure good lighting and a clear camera view. Try again by adjusting your face position.

Are there any limits on earning SB Coins?

+

Yes, there are daily and monthly earning limits to ensure fair use. Check your dashboard for your current earnings.

Can I transfer SE Coins to another user?

+

No, SE Coins are non-transferable. They can only be redeemed for rewards through your own account.
